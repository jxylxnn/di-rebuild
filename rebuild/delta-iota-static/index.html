Musical Achievement & Brotherhood

Welcome to the official website of the Delta Iota Chapter. We are dedicated to serving the band program and fostering a brotherhood built on music, service, and leadership.

Since our founding, we have strived for the highest in musical achievement and the advancement of the college band. Our legacy is built on the hard work and dedication of brothers who came before us.
