The Delta Iota Chapter

Welcome to the official website of the Delta Iota Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi, National Honorary Band Fraternity. We are dedicated to serving the band program and fostering a brotherhood built on music, service, and leadership.

Since our founding, we have strived for the highest in musical achievement and the advancement of the college band.
